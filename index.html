How to Play

Hold Your Phone Upright

Keep your phone vertical, microphone facing up toward your mouth at about 15–20cm distance.

Hum Like a Kazoo

Close your lips and hum steadily through your nose — like humming "mmmmm". This activates your oral and jaw muscles.

TARGET

Match the Falling Notes

Notes fall from the top. When a note reaches the Target Zone, hum steadily to score. Keep your pitch stable for bonus accuracy!

🎵
Stable! Erratic

Watch Your Stability

The Stability Thermometer shows how steady your hum is. Aim for the green zone — consistent muscle control = better rehabilitation!
